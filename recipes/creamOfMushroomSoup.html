Mushroom Soup

Description

Recipe for Cream Of Mushroom Soup

Ingredients:

  • Button Mushrooms
  • Vegtable Broth
  • Cream

Steps:

  1. Cook Mushrooms in a pot with Vegtable Broth
  2. Cool Mushrooms and broth, then add to a blender
  3. Blend Mushrooms to the desired texture
  4. Add blended Mushrooms and broth to a pot and simmer for 5 mins
  5. Add Cream, black pepper, salt and Enjoy!
