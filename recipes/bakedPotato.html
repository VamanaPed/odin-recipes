Baked Potato

Description

Recipe for Microwave Baked Potato

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Grated Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Optional: Black Pepper/Salt

Steps:

  1. Wash the potato
  2. Microwave the potato for 5 mins on medium power
  3. Cut open the hot potato and add the gratted cheese on top
  4. Let potato cool for 2 mins
  5. Add a serving of sour cream on top of the potato
  6. Optional: Garnish with black pepper/salt
  7. Enjoy!
